Vegetation Encroachment Risk Assessment Near Electric Poles

Welcome to our project

As our dependence on electrical power continues to grow, ensuring the reliability and safety of power distribution infrastructure becomes paramount. This project delves into the critical issue of vegetation encroachment near electric poles, a challenge that poses potential risks to both infrastructure integrity and public safety. The unchecked growth of vegetation in proximity to electric poles can lead to disruptions in power supply, fire hazards, and structural damage, necessitating a proactive approach in risk assessment and mitigation.

Our mission is to develop a robust Vegetation Encroachment Risk Assessment system that utilizes cutting-edge technologies to identify and evaluate the potential threats posed by nearby vegetation to electric poles. By implementing advanced data analysis and machine learning techniques, we aim to provide utility companies and maintenance crews with timely and accurate information, enabling them to prioritize and address areas of high risk efficiently. Through this project, we strive to enhance the resilience of power distribution networks and contribute to a safer and more reliable electrical infrastructure.
